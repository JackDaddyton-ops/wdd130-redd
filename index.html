All About Me

Places I've Lived

Because my father served in the military as a radiologist, the footsteps of my childhood can be found all around the USA, and even out of it! The longest I have lived anywhere is 4 years at a time. Moving around is all I know, and every step of the way I had new friends, in a new environment. Being what is termed a “military brat” is a defining characteristic of my story.

My Hobbies

I have always been a big hobby guy, They make up a huge part of what I enjoy and who I am today. I have been able to use my talents to connect with people in all of the places I have lived. Some of my hobbies have changed throughout the years, and many of them are still the same. I like to consider myself a balanced and diverse person as far as my interests and hobbies go.
