My Hobbies

My hobbies have been a big part of my life since the beginning. They have defined who and what I spend my time with. I have always been a talented person, and my desire to be liked in the early days of my life accelerated my talents. My talents have come and gone through the years, but some have stayed consistent. If you want to get along with me, shared hobbies and interests is the fastest way!

Hiking:

My parent are very active people, I have been hiking and camping ever since I can remember. The colorful places I have lived have definitely help encourage me to spend time outside. I have always been an active person, and love the challenge of a wall to climb, or a mountain to summit.

Sports:

As shown in the image I played soccer when I was younger. I always remember my family tossing around a frisbee as a Sunday activity as well. My love for sports drew me to spike-ball when I was in high school which I still play much today. I also participated in cross-country running in high school as well. Surprisingly I picked up basketball and pickleball on my mission as well! I seldom turn down an opportunity to play some sports when the opportunity arises!

Drawing

I have been drawing since I was YOUNG! I would say I was drawing the most in middle-school and beginning of high school. I first loved drawing “rocket ships” when I was very young. Then I had a phase of drawing pirate ships. My final form as far as art has gone is drawing dragons. Sadly I do not do this as much as I used to, but I still have and love it!

Legos/building

was always running around with a lego creation mine when I was a kid. This is where my love for building and designing began. I played with legos until the end of high school. Now my outlet for these skill is designing things in 3D software programs.

Dance

Dancing is one of my biggest hobbies that I still do today! It started when I lived in California when I would watch dancers on YouTube. Marquese Scott is one of my great inspirations. When I was in high school I won 500$ doing a dance routine I came up with. I have made videos, but haven’t posted them (yet). My style incorporates hip hop and animation.
